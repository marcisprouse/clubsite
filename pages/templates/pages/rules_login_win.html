No Purchase Necessary

This promotion is intended to encourage club members to log into the new website for the first time and change their temporary password. There are no goods or services for sale.

Location and Eligible Participants

This contest is exclusively for members of the Coyote Lakes Recreation Club in Surprise, Arizona USA whose dues are current.

Opening Date and Scheduled Termination Date

Sweepstakes begin from October 5, 2021 and concludes at the time of the drawing on November 1, 2021.

Sponsor of the Contest

Coyote Lakes Recreation Club
18860 N Coyote Lakes Pkwy, Surprise, AZ 85378

Prize Description

There is one prize for $50.00 Amazon Gift Card or $50 dues credit.

Prize Awarded

The prize will be available to the winner in the form of an actual gift card that can either be picked up at the Coyote Lakes Recreation Club or mailed to the recipient on the day of winning or a credit to their account.

Selection of Winner

The Winner will be selected using a random number generator to identify one Member ID from the list of eligible participants.

Determination of Winner

Determination of winner will be made on November 1, 2021 based on the electronic drawing that takes place on November 1, 2021.

Announcement of Winner

Winner will be announced on November 1, 2021 and the winner will be posted in the Alerts on the home page of the new website.
