Ways to Pay Dues

Club dues are used to maintain our facility, pay for utilities, and offer great activities to members. We are a volunteer-only organization, so nobody gets paid to do anything. All dues are paid by the members and for the members of the club. Financial reports are available.

Club dues are paid semi-annually, or every six months. A payment in the amount of $300.00 is due both on January 1st and again on July 1st. This is a total of $600.00 for the year.

Currently, we have two electronic options for making your payments for dues. Please continue reading for those options.



Zelle Instructions

Zelle is a way to safely send money through an app for your phone or iPad. Use the buttons above to download the app.

OR

Many banks have Zelle built into their apps to make payments easy. You can check to see if your bank already has it built in here.

Whether you use the Zelle app or use Zelle through your bank's app, you will need the Coyote Lakes Recreation Club's payment email address:

payments@cylrc.org




ECheck Instructions

  • Add the Rec Club as a payee as shown below just like you would any other bill you pay electronically.
