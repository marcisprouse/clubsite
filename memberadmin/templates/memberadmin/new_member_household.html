What Happens Automatically

* Required fields

When you submit this form, the system will automatically create the certificate, create each member, assign member numbers, store each member's working temporary password, subscribe members to the General Newsletter when selected, and prepare the board notification.

If Send board email is checked, the board will automatically receive an email that includes the new certificate number, the new member numbers, any key numbers entered, and the welcome-login details prepared for the household.

After submit, this page will show you the resulting certificate number, member IDs, usernames, temporary passwords, and the generated board and welcome messages.
