This badge form will render a downloadable pdf from which you can print badges using Avery 5390 Templates.

Instructions:

  1. Have Avery 5390 cardstock or just heavy 8.5" x 11" paper in your printer.
  2. Select any number of the members you would like to create a pdf for printable badges and select the color of those badges.
  3. Select the print date. The year of the print date will be the year that appears on the badge.
  4. Select the color of the badge decoration (Choices: Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple, No Color)
  5. Click Submit only once. Wait for about 10 seconds for it to render. You will see a download prompt to download the pdf to your computer.
  6. Open the pdf and print. You may need Adobe Acrobat Reader.
  7. You can also download a report of the badges printed under the Administrator tab --> Excel Reports.
  8. You can report previously printed badges in the Administrative Panel under BADGES --> Badges.
