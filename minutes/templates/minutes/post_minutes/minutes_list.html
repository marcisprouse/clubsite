About the Board

Our club Board of Directors are governed under Article VI of our Coyote Lakes Recreation Club Bylaws. We have nine elected Directors, three of whom serve for a term of one year, three of whom serve for a term of two years, and three of whom serve for a term of three years. The Board of Directors meet quarterly and the meeting is posted on the Club bulletin board. The Executive Offices of the Club are President, Vice-President, Treasurer, and Secretary.
